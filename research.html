Felix's Research Work

My research interests include Network Science, Machine Learning, and Evolutionary Computing. Related research keywords include:

  • Network Science: Robustness; Controllability; Malicious attack; Optimization; Naming game.
  • Machine Learning: Convolutional neural network; Graph neural network; Graph representation learning; Graph embedding; Graph classification; Pyramid pooling.
  • Evolutionary Computation: Non-revisiting; Binary space partitioning tree; Algorithm selection; Benchmark problem generation.

    Previously, I also worked as the PI for the research projects supported by the Lam Woo Research Fund of Lingnan University, the Faculty Research Grant of Lingnan University, the National Natural Science Foundation of＊, and the Open Project Program of the Key Laboratory of System Control and Information Processing of Shanghai Jiao Tong University, among others.
